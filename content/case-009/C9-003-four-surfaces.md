## Capture

Four properties are maintained in parallel, each doing something distinct:

**YouTube (benchanviolin) — the origin surface.** 18+ years of unscripted authentic presence. The record begins in 2007 with a college graduate answering commenters' questions about violin without a script, and continues through parenthood, career, and every inflection point in between. YouTube proves the voice predates any method or system. It is the earliest and longest-running surface.

**YY and Me — narrative audio, vulnerability through story.** A podcast built around YY, the inner child, as the vehicle. Episodes like "Time Enough" and "The First Echo" carry the same inheritance intent in audio narrative form. "The First Echo" describes the podcast's founding intent explicitly: a transmission across time to future children. YY as the inner child who never stopped learning is the character through which the operator's voice carries the most personal material.

**Substack Resonant Patterns — the unassisted voice.** The most defensible and irreplaceable surface. Even when AI is used as a verification layer (Q3), the writing itself is human-authored. The Substack is the only surface where the voice must stand entirely on its own in real time. It is the surface where the operator is most vulnerable to being exposed as performing — and therefore the surface where honest writing is most legible.

**home.yymethod.com — structured reasoning made permanent.** The ADR registry. The full reasoning chains behind decisions, made public, AI-accessible, and permanent. The site makes the methodology visible, not just its outputs. Anyone who wants to understand not just what was decided but why — including the kids, decades later — can find it here.

---

## Why

Each surface transmits something the others cannot:

YouTube transmits continuity of presence — the operator unchanged and changing across two decades of video, unedited by reflection or retrospection.

The podcast transmits the interior — the emotional register that prose tends to flatten, carried by voice and music and a stuffed squirrel character that the kids already know.

The Substack transmits the voice as it exists now — in the act of writing, without AI scaffold, subject to the discipline the operator has built across the four quests.

The case study site transmits the reasoning — the structured record that answers the question "why did you do that?" in a form that can be interrogated by AI tools decades from now when the kids need it.

A single surface cannot do all of this. The redundancy is the resilience: if any one surface disappears, the others carry the corpus forward.

---

## Why-Not

**Why not consolidate to fewer surfaces and do each one better?**
Consolidation would eliminate the things only each surface can carry. A written transcript of a podcast episode is not the podcast. A YouTube video is not an essay. An ADR is not a letter. The form is part of the content. The surfaces are not redundant — they are complementary.

**Why not add more surfaces?**
Adding surfaces without a clear distinct function would dilute without adding. The four current surfaces each have a job the others do not. A fifth surface would need to identify what none of the four currently does. Nothing is currently missing.

---

## Commit

**Decision:** Four surfaces, maintained in parallel, with different AI relationships, different forms, and distinct contributions to the multi-generational corpus. No consolidation. No new surfaces without a clear gap they fill.

**Confidence:** High.
