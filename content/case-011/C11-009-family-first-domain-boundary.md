# C11-009: Family-First Domain Boundary — yymethod.com as Home Surface, Professional Method Elsewhere
**Method:** YY Method™ Home Edition v2.3 — Capture → Why → Why-Not → Commit → Timestamp
**Status:** Decided — Correction
**Date:** 2026-06-06
**Domain:** Boundary
**Depends On:** C11-003, C11-006, C11-007 — corrects the academy/business expansion path without rejecting the professional history
**Freshness Boundary:** Permanent until ownership, employment scope, or audience posture materially changes. Revisit before any public-facing corporate, executive, or employer-adjacent positioning appears on `yymethod.com`.
**Source:** Operator correction. 2026-06-06.

---

## Capture

The operator took the YY Method professional inside his company and has been building it out there under the company's brand. That professional implementation matters, but it does not consume the `yymethod.com` domain or the Home Edition surface. The domain remains personally owned and available for iteration.

The correction is audience boundary. `yymethod.com` can graduate into the full home implementation precisely because it is not the professional surface. Its audience should be families, parents, children participating through family context, and possibly schools someday. Professional use, if it appears at all, is a narrow 1:1 training/advisory carveout. It should not cross into team training, organizational enablement, employer-branded programs, or corporate transformation language.

YY's own personality challenges the corporate path. If the surface becomes corporate, YY starts becoming mascot, brand asset, or tone decoration instead of guide, lens, and emotional operating system. The product should stay protected from that drift.

---

## Why

The professional method can live elsewhere under the employer/company brand. That creates a cleaner separation:

- The company receives the professional implementation built in that context.
- The operator retains `yymethod.com` as the personally owned home surface.
- Families receive a product that is not distorted by enterprise positioning.
- Any professional use remains 1:1, bounded, and non-organizational.
- YY remains free to be YY.

This boundary also strengthens the product. A family-first home surface can develop around AI literacy, wonder, badges, XP, quests, shared learning, and safe graduation from play to reasoning. A corporate surface would pull toward ROI language, workflow automation, leadership enablement, governance packaging, and executive seriousness. Those may be valid elsewhere, but they are not the soul of `yymethod.com`.

The possible school route remains compatible because schools sit closer to family learning than corporate training. A school path would require its own privacy, consent, curriculum, and institutional boundary decisions, but it does not require turning YY into a corporate brand.

The professional carveout is personal instruction, not enterprise delivery. One adult learner can be coached through the method. A CEO or founder can receive 1:1 reasoning support. A parent who is also a professional can learn the method. But the moment the surface trains a team, department, company, or employer cohort, it has crossed into the professional implementation lane.

---

## Why-Not

**Why not use `yymethod.com` for both family and professional audiences?**
Because broad dual-audience pressure will eventually make the surface corporate. The professional buyer has stronger language gravity: outcomes, productivity, transformation, compliance, governance, enablement. That gravity would reshape the family product even if the first version tried to keep both. A 1:1 carveout is different because it is personal instruction, not a market posture.

**Why not abandon the professional method entirely?**
The professional method exists and has value. The correction is not rejection. It is placement. Professional work belongs under the company/employer context or another explicitly professional surface, not the personal Home Edition domain.

**Why not keep the academy language?**
Academy can remain only if it means family learning or school-compatible learning. If "academy" starts implying executive training, corporate upskilling, or professional advisory, it violates this boundary.

---

## Commit

**Decision:** `yymethod.com` and YY Method™ Home Edition are family-first surfaces. They may grow toward family AI literacy, parent-child learning, gamified quests, child-safe creative sandboxes, and possibly school-oriented learning. Professional use is limited to 1:1 training/advisory and must not become team, department, company, employer, or cohort training. The professional YY Method implementation developed inside the operator's company remains acknowledged but separate. YY's personality is a guardrail against corporate drift.

**Confidence:** High.
