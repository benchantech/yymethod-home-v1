---

## Capture

The doctrine in `yymethod-home` is structurally useful but emotionally under-anchored. It explains judgment, provenance, correction, and durable reasoning, but it does not yet carry the soul of the original YY from `yyand.me`: the reddish-brown stuffed squirrel with the Y-shaped nose, the first storytelling partner, the bridge between childhood imagination and adult creative identity.

`yysworld` attempted to make YY central, but the operator did not participate directly in the storytelling. The result preserved some scaffolding and some character traits, but not the full lived personality. The real source is still `yyand.me`: YY as companion, comic interruption, emotional thread, inner-child keeper, and narrative lens.

The decision is to promote real YY from brand decoration to first-class viewpoint. YY is not the mascot standing next to the method. YY is the lens through which the method can be taught without losing wonder.

---

## Why

The YY Method grew from the same origin as YY: childhood imagination disciplined into adult structure. If the method is presented only as doctrine, it becomes dry. If YY is used only as a cute wrapper, he becomes shallow. The power is in the fusion: adult reasoning guided by a character whose authority comes from memory, affection, play, and survival across decades.

YY can hold contradictions the method needs to hold:

- playful but serious
- childlike but not childish
- emotionally warm but structurally rigorous
- family-accessible but professionally credible
- mythic enough for kids, precise enough for adults

This gives `yymethod-home` a soul without weakening the doctrine.

---

## Why-Not

**Why not keep YY as a mascot and let the method stand alone?**
A mascot decorates a system. A viewpoint shapes a system. The operator's insight is that the missing element is not illustration; it is perspective. The method needs to be taught as if YY is looking at the adult world and asking why the grown-ups keep losing the thread.

**Why not let AI-generated `yysworld` canon define YY going forward?**
`yysworld` is useful as an experimental branch, but it cannot outrank lived canon. The operator's direct relationship with YY is the source of authority. AI-generated versions may extend, test, or branch YY, but they cannot replace the original lens.

**Why not invent a new academy guide to avoid baggage?**
A new guide would be cleaner but weaker. YY's force comes from being real within the family's story. The baggage is the point: memory, inheritance, play, aging, parenthood, and adult work all meet in YY.

---

## Commit

**Decision:** Real YY from `yyand.me` is promoted to first-class viewpoint for YY Method™ Home Edition. Future academy, gamification, lesson, and product surfaces should treat YY as guide, lens, and emotional operating system, not as decorative mascot. `yysworld` remains a branch/sandbox lineage; `yyand.me` remains the canonical emotional source.

**Confidence:** High.
