## Capture

The emerging architecture has two linked fields:

- `yyworld` is the kids' safe sandbox: imagination, character play, experiments, low-stakes worldbuilding, and family-supervised creative exploration.
- `yymethod-home` is the graduation field: the place where real ideas become reasoning records, philosophy, training modules, and eventually product engine components.

The system needs a pathway between them. Not every sandbox idea graduates. Graduation happens when an idea becomes explainable, traceable, useful, and durable enough to enter the method.

---

## Why

The sandbox protects play. The graduation field protects doctrine.

Children and families need a place where ideas can be messy, funny, private, and unfinished. That is `yyworld` and the related child-originated studio architecture. But the LLC mission also needs a place where ideas are distilled into teachable form. That is `yymethod-home`.

Graduation gives the family a powerful lesson: imagination can become structure. A story beat can become a design principle. A mistake can become a scar. A playful quest can become a real mental model for working with AI.

---

## Why-Not

**Why not merge sandbox and graduation field into one product?**
The two fields have different safety, privacy, and seriousness requirements. Merging them would either make the sandbox too formal or the doctrine too loose.

**Why not keep kids' sandbox work completely separate from the method?**
Complete separation would lose the core family-learning insight. The pathway is how children see that play can mature into real ideas without being exploited or prematurely published.

**Why not graduate everything?**
Graduation requires selection. The method should teach discernment: not every idea becomes doctrine, and that is healthy.

---

## Commit

**Decision:** `yyworld` is the safe sandbox and `yymethod-home` is the graduation field. Ideas may move from sandbox to method only when they are family-approved, appropriately private/public, explainable, traceable, and useful as teaching material. Graduation is a meaningful step, not an automatic pipeline.

**Confidence:** High.
