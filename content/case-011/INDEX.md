## What This Case Is

A structured knowledge artifact capturing the mission crystallization for YY Method™ Home Edition: the doctrine of the method should be taught through the lens of the real YY from `yyand.me`, promoted from mascot/reference to first-class viewpoint.

The goal is not to make a child-directed product. The goal is to build an adult-positioned family learning system where parents can learn AI from the ground up while bringing their families into a shared, gamified world. `yyworld` remains the kids' safe sandbox for imagination and character play. `yymethod-home` becomes the graduation field where sandboxed ideas become real reasoning, philosophy, and product engine design.

The case documents seventeen decision domains:

1. **Canon** — Real YY from `yyand.me` becomes the first-class guide.
2. **Voice** — Childlike wonder is carried through adult syntax.
3. **Positioning** — Family-shareable, adult-directed, not kids-only.
4. **Progression** — Badges, XP, quests, and family victories become the learning loop.
5. **Pathway** — `yyworld` is sandbox; `yymethod-home` is graduation field.
6. **Business** — Ben Chan Tech graduates toward a family-first learning academy model.
7. **Worldview** — The YY forest metaphor can mature for families and 1:1 adult learners without becoming corporate.
8. **Funding** — Client advisory work funds doctrine extraction without taking over the mission.
9. **Boundary** — `yymethod.com` remains the personally owned home surface; professional use is limited to 1:1 training/advisory.
10. **Seed** — The fantasy theme is immutable because it grows from the operator's real childhood thinking modes.
11. **Adaptation** — New Game Plus maps onto current gamification and social proof without replacing the seed.
12. **Architecture** — yysworld's code structure becomes the early construction kernel for the new yymethod.
13. **Consent** — The founder-parent's non-coercion posture becomes the first system under test.
14. **Disclosure** — Upside is named clearly without converting future benefit into pressure.
15. **Privacy** — The child's journey is the private key; YY Method is the public key.
16. **Lens** — YY narrates private learning at safe distance and teaches self-narration.
17. **Expansion** — The first child may create a founder-led golden path only by personal opt-in.

## The Founding Argument in One Sentence

The real YY is not decoration; he is the emotional operating system that lets adult AI doctrine remain playful enough for families to enter and serious enough for professionals to trust.

## Cascade Rules

If YY's canonical characterization changes in `yyand.me` or `yysworld` → revisit C11-001 and C11-002 before changing public teaching voice  
If any surface begins soliciting, profiling, or collecting from children directly → revisit C11-003 immediately; the adult-directed posture is no longer intact  
If gamification starts rewarding shallow completion instead of real reasoning → revisit C11-004; XP must measure demonstrated cognitive moves, not clicks  
If `yyworld` becomes public or account-based for minors → revisit C11-005 against Case 010's privacy and authorship architecture  
If Ben Chan Tech client work begins driving doctrine content instead of funding extraction → revisit C11-008; advisory work is a funding source and field laboratory, not the owner of the mission  
If business training drops the YY forest/fantasy metaphor entirely → revisit C11-007; seriousness may graduate, but the thematic through-line should remain visible
If `yymethod.com` begins addressing teams, departments, companies, employer cohorts, or organization-wide professional training audiences → revisit C11-009 immediately; the Home Edition audience boundary has broken  
If a school route becomes concrete → file a new case or ADR for education privacy, curriculum posture, institutional consent, and child data boundaries before implementation
If any future surface treats fantasy as generic branding rather than truthful maturation of childhood thinking modes → C11-010 is violated; revise the surface, not the seed
If gamification or social-proof norms shift away from badges, XP, creator proof, visible logs, or AI personalization → revisit C11-011; the seed stays fixed, but the interface adapts
If `yymethod-home` gains runtime learner/account/progression state, generated quest surfaces, or a public `yyworld` graduation path → revisit C11-012 before implementation; yysworld's kernel pattern should shape the architecture early, not be retrofitted after slop appears
If the founder-parent cannot accept "no" as a successful outcome → do not introduce the child-led AI ledger; C11-013 is not satisfied
If upside language starts sounding like obligation, future regret, family duty, or parent disappointment → revise the invitation before any child sees it; C11-014 is violated
If a public projection cannot be rederived from the private source without exposing that source → do not publish; C11-015 is violated
If YY narration begins replacing the child's authorship or evaluating the child directly → revisit C11-016; YY is the lens, not the owner of the journey
If siblings, trusted families, adults, or parent-child learners are invited before opt-in/opt-out mechanics are proven → revisit C11-017; the golden path models agency, not obligation
