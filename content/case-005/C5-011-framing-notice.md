## Capture

This case captures a live family housing decision. The reasoning structure, constraint hierarchy, and decision logic are preserved at full fidelity. Identifying details — family composition, specific financial figures, relationship specifics, and personal network references — are abstracted or generalized.

The method is the subject. The family is not.

---

## Why

Case 005 involves active financial planning, minor children, and personal relationships. Publishing exact figures, family composition details, or identifiable network references would expose the household beyond what is necessary to illustrate the method.

The YY Method™ Home Edition publishes reasoning chains, not personal disclosures. The value of this case lies in the structure of the decision-making — the constraint stack, the optionality reasoning, the integrity kill — not in the specific numbers or individuals involved.

---

## Why-Not

**Why not publish at full fidelity?**
The household's financial state and family composition are not methodologically necessary at the specific level. Order-of-magnitude figures preserve the reasoning without exposing the actuals.

**Why not suppress the financial context entirely?**
The opportunity cost realization (C5-002) is a load-bearing part of the case — it is what forced the re-evaluation. Removing it entirely would hollow out the reasoning chain. The solution is to preserve the scale (7-figure compounding) without disclosing the specific delta.

---

## Abstraction Rules Applied

- **Family composition:** Not enumerated. "Kids are split" — some ambivalent, some for, some against — without count or fraction.
- **Financial figures:** Described as order-of-magnitude and illustrative. The 7-figure compounding conclusion is preserved; the specific capital delta is not.
- **Personal relationships:** Tenants described as "trusted individuals in the operator's network," not by relationship type.
- **Financing sources:** Third-party financing described as "personal financing from a known party," not by identity of the party.
- **Structure preserved:** All constraint logic, dependency chains, integrity kills, and operating decisions are captured at full resolution.
