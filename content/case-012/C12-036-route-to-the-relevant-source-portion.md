## Capture

Send users to the exact relevant segment rather than a full long video.

## Why

- lower search burden;
- better fit under stress;
- clearer provenance;
- easier Coach routing;
- improved accessibility.

## Why-Not

Short modular clips may support looping and exact physical demonstration.

Describe segment-level routing without publishing production metadata or route maps.
