## Capture

AI praise often sounds like:

- “Great job!”
- “That’s an excellent insight.”
- “You’re making amazing progress.”

This may feel hollow, patronizing, or inaccurate.

Avoid generic encouragement.

## Why

This decision matters because prefer Precise Reflection Over Praise constrains the product toward continuity, evidence, and human judgment rather than broad AI helpfulness.
