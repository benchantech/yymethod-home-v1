## Capture

Potential identities included concepts such as:

- Violin Companion;
- Between Lessons;
- Violin Coach;
- Stand Partner;
- page-turning companion;
- memory or practice partner.

“Stand Partner” initially functioned as a description of how the AI should behave.

It later became strong enough to carry the product.

Adopt **Violin Stand Partner** as the product name and organizing relationship.

## Why

- it is native to the violin domain;
- it describes relationship rather than authority;
- it implies proximity and continuity;
- it respects ensemble culture;
- it supports the broader Benchantech “stand” motif;
- it can mean both standing beside and withstanding judgment.

- “Companion” was generic;
- “Coach” implied broad authority;
- “Between Lessons” described timing but not relationship;
- “Practice” narrowed the product too much;
- “AI” in the title foregrounded the mechanism instead of the trust model.

## Why-Not

- “Companion” was generic;
- “Coach” implied broad authority;
- “Between Lessons” described timing but not relationship;
- “Practice” narrowed the product too much;
- “AI” in the title foregrounded the mechanism instead of the trust model.

- trademark strategy;
- domain acquisition plan;
- unannounced naming tests;
- internal launch naming variants.

## Commit

**Decision:** Adopt **Violin Stand Partner** as the product name and organizing relationship.

The decision becomes a constraint on future product behavior and public positioning.
