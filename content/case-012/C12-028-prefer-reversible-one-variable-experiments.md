## Capture

- change bow speed but not pressure;
- alter one fingering;
- test one posture cue;
- compare one phrase under two conditions.

When experimentation is appropriate, change one variable and preserve a path back.

## Why

Multiple simultaneous changes destroy evidence.

## Why-Not

Not every instrument-facing action is reversible. Physical action requires stronger authorization.

---

## Commit

**Decision:** When experimentation is appropriate, change one variable and preserve a path back.

Not every instrument-facing action is reversible. Physical action requires stronger authorization.
