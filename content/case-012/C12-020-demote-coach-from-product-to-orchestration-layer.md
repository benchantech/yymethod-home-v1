## Capture

An unlimited conversational coach could handle everything.

Coach is powerful but should not become the whole product.

## Why

This decision matters because demote Coach From Product to Orchestration Layer constrains the product toward continuity, evidence, and human judgment rather than broad AI helpfulness.

## Why-Not

- chat is poor at visible parallel state;
- users lose track of commitments;
- the AI may drift;
- memory becomes opaque;
- source provenance becomes unclear;
- the daily ritual becomes optional rather than structural.

## Commit

**Decision:** Coach is powerful but should not become the whole product.

The decision becomes a constraint on future product behavior and public positioning.

**Confidence:** Medium-high for public architecture; implementation details remain private and subject to launch evidence.
