## Capture

The initial self-directed audience includes returning adults and developing players who can articulate practice concerns but need continuity and structure.

## Why

- they can describe problems;
- they may have prior training;
- they often lack current support;
- they can evaluate suggestions;
- they may already consume Ben’s content.

It ignored the repeated parent-support problem that shaped Ben’s teaching experience.

## Why-Not

The rejected path would make begin With Returning and Developing Players too easy to treat as an implementation detail instead of a public boundary. The ADR preserves the tradeoff explicitly so later product work cannot silently widen authority.

## Commit

**Decision:** The initial self-directed audience includes returning adults and developing players who can articulate practice concerns but need continuity and structure.

The decision becomes a constraint on future product behavior and public positioning.

**Confidence:** Medium-high for public architecture; implementation details remain private and subject to launch evidence.
