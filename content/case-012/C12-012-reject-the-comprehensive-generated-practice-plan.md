## Capture

A complete plan seems personalized and valuable:

- warm-up;
- scales;
- repertoire;
- technique;
- review;
- performance preparation.

Do not make the daily product a large AI-generated practice plan.

## Why

A complete plan seems personalized and valuable:

- warm-up;
- scales;
- repertoire;
- technique;
- review;
- performance preparation.

## Why-Not

- it creates cognitive load;
- it can become generic;
- it encourages AI overreach;
- it creates too many simultaneous obligations;
- it may not reflect current energy or available time;
- it recreates the burden users already avoid.

## Commit

**Decision:** Do not make the daily product a large AI-generated practice plan.

The Plan view may still organize larger arcs, but the Today view remains narrow.
