## Capture

1. AI gathers observations.
2. User confirms relevant facts.
3. A reviewed Compact establishes possible eligibility.
4. A deterministic tree controls permitted action.
5. The user performs or declines.
6. The system records result and reroutes.

The AI may gather context conversationally. Concrete physical authorization must be bounded by deterministic logic.

## Why

Generative AI is useful for language and clarification but should not improvise the final physical action boundary.

> Conversational intake may be probabilistic. Concrete authorization is bounded and deterministic.

## Why-Not

- decision trees;
- route transitions;
- eligibility checks;
- failure states;
- exact question ordering;
- testing thresholds.
