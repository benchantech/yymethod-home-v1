## Capture

The primary daily action should generally be completable in five minutes.

## Why

- low activation energy;
- meaningful enough to produce evidence;
- compatible with busy families;
- easy to market;
- avoids the all-or-nothing practice mentality;
- supports repeated use;
- allows continuation without requiring it.

> Everyone has five minutes. The app helps make those five minutes count.

## Why-Not

Exact timer behavior, action-generation rules, and completion thresholds.

---

## Commit

**Decision:** The primary daily action should generally be completable in five minutes.

Five minutes is a minimum commitment, not a cap.

> Everyone has five minutes. The app helps make those five minutes count.
