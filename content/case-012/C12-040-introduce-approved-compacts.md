## Capture

Ben’s reviewed lived-experience judgments should be encoded in bounded knowledge units.

## Why

A broad blueprint could not safely contain every domain judgment.

The blueprint should remain stable while Approved Compacts grow.

The moat is not merely the concept of a Compact. It is:

- the reviewed Compact library;
- the distinctions inside it;
- the tests;
- the source linkage;
- the route eligibility logic;
- the accumulated corrections.

## Why-Not

Production Compacts, exact conditions, edge cases, thresholds, and mappings.

---

## Commit

**Decision:** Ben’s reviewed lived-experience judgments should be encoded in bounded knowledge units.

The decision becomes a constraint on future product behavior and public positioning.

**Public boundary:** This ADR publishes the judgment and product boundary while withholding exact prompts, routes, thresholds, schemas, tests, and other reproducible implementation details where applicable.

**Confidence:** Medium-high for public architecture; implementation details remain private and subject to launch evidence.
