## Capture

One active concern would make state easier.

Allow multiple active practice pages.

## Why

This decision matters because reject the Single-Thread Assumption constrains the product toward continuity, evidence, and human judgment rather than broad AI helpfulness.

## Why-Not

Violin practice is naturally parallel:

- technique;
- repertoire;
- sound;
- intonation;
- memory;
- performance preparation;
- maintenance;
- teacher questions.

Even one piece may require several distinct pages.

## Commit

**Decision:** Allow multiple active practice pages.

This creates the need for explicit per-page state and branch separation.
