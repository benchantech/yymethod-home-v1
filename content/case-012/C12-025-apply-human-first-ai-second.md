## Capture

The AI can execute cognitive and organizational work but cannot take final judgment from the user or appropriate human authority.

## Why

> AI may organize, retrieve, compare, and propose. The player, parent, teacher, or qualified professional retains judgment.

The same observation can require different action depending on:

- player history;
- teacher plan;
- physical condition;
- instrument condition;
- emotional state;
- performance timeline.

## Why-Not

The rejected path would make apply Human First, AI Second too easy to treat as an implementation detail instead of a public boundary. The ADR preserves the tradeoff explicitly so later product work cannot silently widen authority.

## Commit

**Decision:** The AI can execute cognitive and organizational work but cannot take final judgment from the user or appropriate human authority.

> AI may organize, retrieve, compare, and propose. The player, parent, teacher, or qualified professional retains judgment.

The app should not hide its uncertainty behind fluency.
